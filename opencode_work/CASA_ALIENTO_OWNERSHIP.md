## STRUCTURAL INDICATORS (No External Lookup Needed)

### Entity Structure
- **Seller:** Casa Aliento Mercy House CHDO LLC (100% controlled by Mercy House CHDO Inc)
- **Buyer:** Casa Aliento, LP
- **CHDO equity in buyer:** 0.0049% ($100 out of ~$2M+ total LP equity)

### Related-Party Indicators

| Indicator | Strength |
|-----------|----------|
| CHDO entity and LP share the same name ("Casa Aliento") | **High** — common in LIHTC structures where nonprofit CHDO creates a for-profit LP with same project name |
| CHDO held equity in BOTH the seller AND the buyer | **Confirmed** — 0.0049% in Casa Aliento LP |
| 90% seller financing ($13.5M on $15M sale) | **High** — unprecedented for arm's-length deal |
| Deferred principal/interest for 55 years (Note 3) | **High** — effectively a grant disguised as a note |
| Sale closed same month as Ontario liquidation (June 2023) | **Medium** — concurrent asset disposition pattern |

### LP Partner Structure (LIHTC Standard)
In a typical LIHTC deal:
- The "LP" in Casa Aliento, LP is the **tax credit investor** (e.g., a bank, insurance company, or syndicator)
- The investor puts up equity (~$2M) in exchange for 99.9951% ownership and 99.99% of tax credits
- The CHDO (0.0049%) retains nominal ownership for compliance
- The **General Partner** (GP) of the LP is the entity that actually controls the project
