## VIOLATIONS

### California False Claims Act (Gov. Code § 12650 et seq.):
- False certifications of payroll necessity for PPP loans
- False representations of employee count and business operations

### California Unfair Competition Law (Bus. & Prof. Code § 17200):
- Unlawful, unfair, and fraudulent business practices
- Obtaining PPP funds through fraudulent misrepresentations

### California Penal Code § 186.1 — Money Laundering:
- PPP proceeds layered through California LLC structures
- Real estate acquisition in California (Orange County properties)
