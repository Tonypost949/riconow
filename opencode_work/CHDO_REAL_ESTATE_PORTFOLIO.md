## KEY TRANSACTIONS

### 1. Casa Aliento / Vagabond Inn — SOLD ($15M)
- Property: Former Vagabond Inn, Oxnard, CA
- Sold: September 2023
- Buyer: Casa Aliento LP
- Price: $15,000,000
- Structure: $13,500,000 seller carryback promissory note (90% financing)
- Loss: ~$11,500
- Red flags: 90% seller financing to unknown LP; CHDO held only 0.0049% equity; deferred principal/interest for 55 years

### 2. Ontario Contract — TERMINATED (Jun 2023)
- Contract with City of Ontario terminated June 30, 2023
- Quitclaim deed: all property assigned to Ontario Housing Authority
- Notes payable cancelled
- Assets with NBV of ~$1,916,000 assigned
- Gain on disposal: ~$717,000
- Red flag: Same month as Vagabond Inn sale closing — pattern of simultaneous liquidation

### 3. CM CHDO / Mesa Vista — Costa Mesa ($7.9M)
- Incorporated: October 4, 2022
- Purpose: Rehabilitate former motel into affordable housing
- 55-year ground lease with County of Orange (Dec 8, 2022)
- Funding: $7,900,000 loan from Century Housing Corporation
- County of Orange + City of Costa Mesa: $2,000,000 + $1,304,639 loans (max $7,700,000)
- City of Costa Mesa grants: $1,457,000 (2024) + $2,043,000 (2023)
- **Feb 2024: $6,500,000 paid down to Century using County of Orange grant funds**
- Red flag: Public grant funds used to retire private lender debt

### 4. Additional Real Estate Sale (May 2024)
- Unspecified property sold for ~$927,000
- Gain: ~$296,000

## CENTURY HOUSING CORPORATION RELATIONSHIP

Century Housing appears as lender on multiple CHDO projects:
- Mesa Vista: $7,900,000 loan (partially paid down $6.5M by County of Orange grants)
- Vagabond Inn: Prior loan paid off at sale ($1,500,000)
- Multiple other notes payable

**Pattern:** Public grant funds → Mercy House CHDO → Century Housing debt service → private lender enriched by public money

## STRUCTURAL RED FLAGS

1. **Minimal equity, maximum control:** CHDO holds 0.002%-0.0049% equity in each LP but controls all transactions
2. **Public-to-private pipeline:** Government grants/loans → CHDO → private lender payoffs (Century)
3. **Simultaneous liquidation:** Ontario contract cancellation + Vagabond Inn sale both June 2023
4. **Seller financing pattern:** $13.5M carryback (90%) on Casa Aliento — effectively retained beneficial interest
5. **Same lender across projects:** Century Housing Corporation appears on 3+ projects
