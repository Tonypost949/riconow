# CPRA REQUEST — Orange County Social Services Agency
## CPS Removal Data vs. IV-E Federal Drawdown — Ghost Billing Investigation
### Date: June 22, 2026

**To:** Orange County Social Services Agency — Records Division
**From:** [Requester]
**Subject:** California Public Records Act Request — CPS Removal Statistics & IV-E Funding Reconciliation

Pursuant to the California Public Records Act (Government Code § 7920.000 et seq.), I request the following records:

### 1. CPS REMOVAL DATA (FY2018-FY2025)

For each fiscal year 2018 through 2025:

a. **Total CPS referrals received** (calls, reports, walk-ins)
b. **Total removals** (children removed from home by county)
c. **Placement destinations** broken down by:
   - Foster family home (licensed)
   - Group home / congregate care
   - Shelter / Navigation Center (including HBNC at 17631 Cameron Ln, HB)
   - Kinship care (relative placement)
   - Tribal placement (ICWA-qualified)
   - Out-of-state placement
   - Juvenile hall / probation crossover
d. **Length of stay** statistics by placement type
e. **Reunification rate** — how many children returned to parents within 12 months

### 2. IV-E FEDERAL DRAW-DOWN DATA

f. Total Title IV-E (42 USC 671-679b) reimbursement claimed per fiscal year
g. Per-diem rate charged to HHS for each placement category
h. Any HHS/ACF audit findings, disallowances, or corrective action plans regarding OC's IV-E claims

### 3. SPECIFIC LOCATION DATA

i. List of all group homes, shelters, and congregate care facilities used for CPS placements within Huntington Beach, specifically including any facilities at:
   - 17631 Cameron Lane, HB 92647 (HBNC Navigation Center)
   - 17642 Beach Blvd, HB 92647
   - 17672 Beach Blvd, HB 92647

j. Number of children placed at each facility per year

### 4. DEATH DATA

k. Number of child deaths occurring in CPS custody or within 30 days of placement by year
l. Cause of death classifications for children who died in placement (medical, environmental, abuse, neglect)
