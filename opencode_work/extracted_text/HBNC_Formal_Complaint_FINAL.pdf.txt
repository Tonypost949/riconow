FORMAL NOTICE OF VIOLATION
RECIPIENTS:
California State Auditor; CA Dept. of Housing (HCD); OC Grand Jury; Investigative Press
SUBJECT:
NOTICE OF VIOLATION AND REPORT OF FATALITIES: 17642 BEACH BLVD
1. ILLEGAL DETENTION & SYSTEMIC ABUSE
The Huntington Beach Navigation Center (HBNC) functions as an unlicensed, gated detention center.
Residents are prohibited from 'walk-outs,' essentially being held without judicial or medical authority. This
policy is enforced on the historic Yamada property, a site of former Japanese-American internment.
2. REPORT OF FATALITIES
We are reporting that homeless and disabled citizens have died at this facility. Those remaining describe their
survival as a matter of luck in the face of what is being characterized as a state-wide targeted removal and
neglect of the disabled homeless population.
3. FINANCIAL FRAUD (LMIHAF & SB 535)
6.1 million dollars of permanent housing money (LMIHAF) was used for this temporary tent. Additionally,
environmental justice funds (SB 535) for this Disadvantaged Community (DAC) have been diverted while
residents are stuck for years in a polluted industrial zone.
DEMAND:
Immediate forensic audit, criminal investigation into facility deaths, and the immediate cessation of illegal
detention policies.
