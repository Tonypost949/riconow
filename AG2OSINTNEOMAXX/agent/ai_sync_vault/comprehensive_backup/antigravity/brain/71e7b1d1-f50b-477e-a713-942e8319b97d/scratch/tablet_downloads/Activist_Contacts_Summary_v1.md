## Data Collection Methodology

### Search Strategy
Conducted systematic web searches across multiple categories:

1. **Environmental Organizations** - Climate, conservation, wildlife, coastal protection
2. **Social Justice & Civil Rights** - Immigration rights, racial justice, ACLU chapters
3. **Community Activism Groups** - Volunteer organizations, community service
4. **Animal Rights & Welfare** - Wildlife protection, humane society, rescue organizations
5. **Labor Unions & Worker Advocacy** - Worker rights, union organizing, labor education
6. **Immigration Rights** - Immigrant justice, refugee services, legal advocacy
7. **LGBTQ+ Rights** - Community centers, civil rights organizations
8. **Women's Rights & Empowerment** - Women's advocacy, gender justice, girls' education
9. **Youth Development** - Mentoring programs, Boys & Girls Clubs, youth organizing
10. **Housing & Homeless Services** - Homelessness prevention, housing advocacy
11. **Faith-Based & Interfaith** - Religious social justice organizations
12. **Health & Wellness Advocacy** - Women's health, disease-specific advocacy
13. **Individual Activists** - Climate activists, environmental justice leaders

### Sources Used
- **Web Search:** Google web search with geographic targeting
- **Official Websites:** City of Huntington Beach community groups directory
- **Nonprofit Databases:** OC Nonprofit Central, OC Nonprofit databases
- **Social Media:** Facebook, Instagram, Twitter profiles for organizations
- **Organizational Websites:** Direct visits to 85+ organization websites
- **Local News:** LA Times, Voice of OC articles about local activism
- **University Resources:** UCI Labor Center, Chapman University connections
