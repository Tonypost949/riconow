1.  **Sheriff-Only Policing:** Lack of localized municipal audit and oversight.
2.  **Environmental Justice Overlays:** Placement of shelters on unsealed Superfund, industrial, or coal/agricultural runoff sites, allowing operators to bypass environmental clearance costs while pocketing clean-up grants.
3.  **Health Billing NPI Vacuum:** Complete absence of registered National Provider Identifier (NPI) health-billing verification networks, allowing fraudulent or unrendered telehealth/clinical support services to be invoiced with impunity.
